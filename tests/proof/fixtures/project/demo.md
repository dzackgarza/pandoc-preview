# Geometry of Numbers — Café

A *naïve* introduction to the geometry of numbers.

The lattice points are counted by the following steps:

1. Choose a symmetric convex body.
2. Apply Minkowski's theorem.
3. Minkowski bound

The Basel sum is $\zeta(2) = \pi^2/6$.

> [!NOTE] Lattice note
>
> Minkowski bounds the points.
